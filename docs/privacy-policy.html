Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

The Visionaries Fan Control ("the app") is a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) companion app developed as a University of California, Irvine (UCI) senior project for the NanoESP32_Fan prototype.

Information We Collect

We collect limited information needed to provide app features:

  • Account information: email address and authentication data managed through Firebase Authentication.
  • App preference data: selected language preference stored in Firebase Realtime Database.
  • Bluetooth data: local BLE connection state and device interaction needed to control the fan hardware.

We do not collect health data, financial data, or precise location data for fan control features.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • authenticate users,
  • save language preferences,
  • enable BLE fan control and connection status,
  • maintain app security and reliability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may use service providers (such as Google Firebase) to provide authentication and database functionality.

Data Retention

Account and preference data are retained while your account remains active or as needed to provide app functionality. You may request account deletion by contacting us.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect account and app data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

International Users

If you use the app outside your home country, your information may be processed in regions where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
